# Ask My Notes

### A Subject-Scoped Intellectual Workspace for Students.

**Ask My Notes** is a premium, AI-powered study ecosystem designed to transform your personal academic material into an interactive knowledge base. Unlike general-purpose chatbots, Ask My Notes operates within the strict boundaries of your curriculum, ensuring every answer is grounded in your specific lecture notes, textbooks, and research.

---

## What is Ask My Notes?

Ask My Notes is built on the philosophy of **"Precision over Presence."** It is not a broad search engine; it is a context-aware intellectual partner. By indexing your documents subject-by-subject, it allows you to transition between complex domains while maintaining perfect source integrity.

It is designed for students who need more than just answers—they need **verification**, **transparency**, and **active engagement** with their own materials.

---

## Core Features
### 📂 Subject-Scoped Indexing
The AI's "brain" shifts along with your focus. When you switch subjects (e.g., from *Organic Chemistry* to *European History*), the AI instantly re-scopes its entire context to index *only* the notes in that specific environment. No cross-contamination, just pure subject focus.

### 🔍 Verified Source Citations
Every response includes direct citations from your notes. This eliminates the "black box" of AI, giving you the power to verify every claim against your original material.

### Adaptive Study Dashboard
A central hub for mastering any subject. Instead of simple reading, transition into a premium card-based dashboard that offers multiple training paths:

- **Knowledge Gap Map**: A live visualization of your proficiency. It identifies your **Strong**, **Weak**, and **Unknown** topics based on your performance, letting you "Target Weakness" with surgical precision.
- **Practice Quiz & Flashcards**: AI-generated Multiple Choice Questions, conceptual Short Answers with examiner feedback, and interactive flippable Flashcards.
- **AI Oral Examiner (Viva)**: Defend your knowledge in a high-stakes, 3-question structured viva session. The AI acts as a strict examiner, grilling you on details only found in your notes.

### Voice-First Learning
Speak to your notes. With integrated Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS), you can conduct entire study sessions hands-free. Ask questions and hear the AI tutor respond with cited evidence.

### The "Not Found" Guarantee
Ask My Notes is built for academic rigor. If the answer to your question isn't present in your uploaded notes, the system will transparently admit it: *"Not found in your notes for [Subject]."* This ensures you only study what is actually part of your syllabus.
